# Chapter Gate Checklist

Before prose:
- [ ] Chapter dramatic need exists.
- [ ] Act/sequence position known.
- [ ] Each scene has target-obstacle-result.
- [ ] Chapter closes with turn or new question.
- [ ] Exposition is timed to current need.

After prose:
- [ ] Opening gives action/sensation/problem before background.
- [ ] At least one situation changes.
- [ ] No scene is pure explanation.
- [ ] The chapter increases pressure or reveals a meaningful contradiction.
