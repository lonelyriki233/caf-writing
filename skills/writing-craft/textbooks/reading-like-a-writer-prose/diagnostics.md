# Diagnostics

## Symptom: language is default
Repair: Interrogate nouns, verbs, adjectives, and omissions for precision/function.
Gate: prose_audit gate: key words earn place.

## Symptom: sentences carry only plot
Repair: Each sentence should shape attention, rhythm, viewpoint, or tension.
Gate: draft gate: sentence function.

## Symptom: paragraphs uniform
Repair: Paragraphs should manage thought/action turns and reader breath.
Gate: prose_audit gate: paragraph rhythm.

## Symptom: emotion overexplained
Repair: Use precise gesture/detail to reveal what character cannot say.
Gate: dialogue_map/prose gate: unsaid embodied.

## Symptom: dialogue polished but false
Repair: Study dialogue for interruption, evasion, rhythm, and subtext rather than neat answers.
Gate: dialogue_map gate: no Q&A dump.

## Symptom: narrator voice vague
Repair: Choose narrative distance and maintain its logic.
Gate: draft gate: distance intentional.

## Symptom: style copying risk
Repair: Extract technique from models without copying phrasing or surface signature.
Gate: revision gate: anti-copy rule.

## Symptom: revision too global
Repair: Revise by close-reading paragraphs for function and pressure.
Gate: revision gate: paragraph-level audit.
