# Action Rules

## word-choice-conscious
Trigger: language is default

Rule: Interrogate nouns, verbs, adjectives, and omissions for precision/function.

SOP gate: prose_audit gate: key words earn place.

## sentence-attention
Trigger: sentences carry only plot

Rule: Each sentence should shape attention, rhythm, viewpoint, or tension.

SOP gate: draft gate: sentence function.

## paragraph-motion
Trigger: paragraphs uniform

Rule: Paragraphs should manage thought/action turns and reader breath.

SOP gate: prose_audit gate: paragraph rhythm.

## gesture-detail
Trigger: emotion overexplained

Rule: Use precise gesture/detail to reveal what character cannot say.

SOP gate: dialogue_map/prose gate: unsaid embodied.

## dialogue-reading
Trigger: dialogue polished but false

Rule: Study dialogue for interruption, evasion, rhythm, and subtext rather than neat answers.

SOP gate: dialogue_map gate: no Q&A dump.

## narration-distance
Trigger: narrator voice vague

Rule: Choose narrative distance and maintain its logic.

SOP gate: draft gate: distance intentional.

## learning-from-models
Trigger: style copying risk

Rule: Extract technique from models without copying phrasing or surface signature.

SOP gate: revision gate: anti-copy rule.

## slow-reading-revision
Trigger: revision too global

Rule: Revise by close-reading paragraphs for function and pressure.

SOP gate: revision gate: paragraph-level audit.
