# Diagnostics

## Symptom: scene opens without directional want
Repair: State or imply a viewpoint goal early; the reader needs an intention to track.
Gate: scene_card gate: target required.

## Symptom: obstacle is solved too easily
Repair: Conflict should complicate, not merely delay; each beat should force a changed tactic.
Gate: scene_card gate: at least one tactic shift.

## Symptom: scene ends where it began
Repair: End with disaster, partial win with cost, revelation, or commitment that changes conditions.
Gate: scene_card gate: result must change situation.

## Symptom: reaction is skipped
Repair: After major turn, provide emotion and thought before decision if pace allows.
Gate: chapter_spec gate: major disaster needs processing beat.

## Symptom: next scene feels arbitrary
Repair: Let sequel decision choose the next scene goal.
Gate: volume_structure gate: scene chain must be causal.

## Symptom: dialogue/actions feel scrambled
Repair: Keep stimulus before response and response before speech/action unless deliberately withholding.
Gate: draft gate: fix reversed causality.

## Symptom: scene viewpoint drifts
Repair: Use one controlling viewpoint per scene unless there is a designed reason to switch.
Gate: draft gate: POV shift must have function.

## Symptom: chapter ends with summary
Repair: End chapters on changed circumstances, question, danger, decision, or emotional turn.
Gate: chapter_spec gate: closing turn required.
