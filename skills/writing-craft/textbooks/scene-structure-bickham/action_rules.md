# Action Rules

## scene-unit-goal
Trigger: scene opens without directional want

Rule: State or imply a viewpoint goal early; the reader needs an intention to track.

SOP gate: scene_card gate: target required.

## conflict-escalation
Trigger: obstacle is solved too easily

Rule: Conflict should complicate, not merely delay; each beat should force a changed tactic.

SOP gate: scene_card gate: at least one tactic shift.

## disaster-or-turn
Trigger: scene ends where it began

Rule: End with disaster, partial win with cost, revelation, or commitment that changes conditions.

SOP gate: scene_card gate: result must change situation.

## sequel-emotion-thought
Trigger: reaction is skipped

Rule: After major turn, provide emotion and thought before decision if pace allows.

SOP gate: chapter_spec gate: major disaster needs processing beat.

## decision-action-bridge
Trigger: next scene feels arbitrary

Rule: Let sequel decision choose the next scene goal.

SOP gate: volume_structure gate: scene chain must be causal.

## stimulus-response-order
Trigger: dialogue/actions feel scrambled

Rule: Keep stimulus before response and response before speech/action unless deliberately withholding.

SOP gate: draft gate: fix reversed causality.

## viewpoint-consistency
Trigger: scene viewpoint drifts

Rule: Use one controlling viewpoint per scene unless there is a designed reason to switch.

SOP gate: draft gate: POV shift must have function.

## chapter-ending-hook
Trigger: chapter ends with summary

Rule: End chapters on changed circumstances, question, danger, decision, or emotional turn.

SOP gate: chapter_spec gate: closing turn required.
