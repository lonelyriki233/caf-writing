# Action Rules

## concept-premise-split
Trigger: idea is interesting but not story

Rule: Separate concept hook from premise execution: who acts, against what, for what stakes.

SOP gate: compass gate: concept + dramatic question required.

## six-core-competencies
Trigger: drafting skips foundation

Rule: Check concept, character, theme, structure, scene, voice as separate layers.

SOP gate: revision gate: identify layer being repaired.

## four-part-structure
Trigger: plot has no architecture

Rule: Use setup, response, attack, resolution with turning milestones.

SOP gate: volume_structure gate: act/part function required.

## first-plot-point
Trigger: inciting change too soft

Rule: First major turn must define the real story path and stakes.

SOP gate: volume_structure gate: plot point 1 forces commitment.

## midpoint-context-shift
Trigger: middle does not transform

Rule: Midpoint should change information, stakes, agency, or reader understanding.

SOP gate: volume_structure gate: midpoint escalation required.

## pinch-points
Trigger: antagonistic pressure disappears

Rule: Show antagonistic force at planned pressure beats.

SOP gate: volume_structure gate: recurring opposition signals.

## theme-through-choice
Trigger: theme stays decorative

Rule: Embed theme in decisions and consequences, not statements.

SOP gate: theme_action gate: action proves theme.

## voice-after-architecture
Trigger: pretty prose before story works

Rule: Polish voice after structural and scene functions are solid.

SOP gate: draft gate: prose last.
