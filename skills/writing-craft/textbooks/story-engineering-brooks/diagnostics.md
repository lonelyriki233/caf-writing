# Diagnostics

## Symptom: idea is interesting but not story
Repair: Separate concept hook from premise execution: who acts, against what, for what stakes.
Gate: compass gate: concept + dramatic question required.

## Symptom: drafting skips foundation
Repair: Check concept, character, theme, structure, scene, voice as separate layers.
Gate: revision gate: identify layer being repaired.

## Symptom: plot has no architecture
Repair: Use setup, response, attack, resolution with turning milestones.
Gate: volume_structure gate: act/part function required.

## Symptom: inciting change too soft
Repair: First major turn must define the real story path and stakes.
Gate: volume_structure gate: plot point 1 forces commitment.

## Symptom: middle does not transform
Repair: Midpoint should change information, stakes, agency, or reader understanding.
Gate: volume_structure gate: midpoint escalation required.

## Symptom: antagonistic pressure disappears
Repair: Show antagonistic force at planned pressure beats.
Gate: volume_structure gate: recurring opposition signals.

## Symptom: theme stays decorative
Repair: Embed theme in decisions and consequences, not statements.
Gate: theme_action gate: action proves theme.

## Symptom: pretty prose before story works
Repair: Polish voice after structural and scene functions are solid.
Gate: draft gate: prose last.
