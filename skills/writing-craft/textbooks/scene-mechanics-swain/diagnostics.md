# Diagnostics

## Symptom: scene feels floaty or reactions seem unmotivated
Repair: Order stimulus before response; make the response physical/emotional/rational before action.
Gate: scene_card gate: every important reaction must have visible stimulus and consequence.

## Symptom: scene only describes events
Repair: Give the viewpoint character a concrete goal, active conflict, and a result that worsens/redirects the situation.
Gate: scene_card gate: no scene passes without goal-conflict-result.

## Symptom: after a disaster the story jumps mechanically
Repair: After a setback, show reaction, dilemma, and decision so the next goal is emotionally caused.
Gate: chapter_spec gate: sequel must produce next action.

## Symptom: characters act because author needs plot
Repair: Link each action to prior motivation and each reaction to observed stimulus.
Gate: draft gate: reject uncaused actions.

## Symptom: chapter has no forward pull
Repair: Pair desire with danger; reader must know what is wanted and what threatens it.
Gate: story_engine gate: want and cost cannot be abstract.

## Symptom: POV lacks immediacy
Repair: Filter information through what the viewpoint character notices under pressure.
Gate: draft gate: viewpoint detail must serve pressure.

## Symptom: obstacle is passive
Repair: Make opposition resist the goal through person, rule, environment, lack, or inner contradiction.
Gate: scene_card gate: obstacle must force tactic change.

## Symptom: chapters feel episodic
Repair: End sequel/scene with a decision that launches the next scene goal.
Gate: chapter_spec gate: closing turn must generate next question/action.
