# Action Rules

## mru-stimulus-response
Trigger: scene feels floaty or reactions seem unmotivated

Rule: Order stimulus before response; make the response physical/emotional/rational before action.

SOP gate: scene_card gate: every important reaction must have visible stimulus and consequence.

## scene-goal-conflict-disaster
Trigger: scene only describes events

Rule: Give the viewpoint character a concrete goal, active conflict, and a result that worsens/redirects the situation.

SOP gate: scene_card gate: no scene passes without goal-conflict-result.

## sequel-reaction-dilemma-decision
Trigger: after a disaster the story jumps mechanically

Rule: After a setback, show reaction, dilemma, and decision so the next goal is emotionally caused.

SOP gate: chapter_spec gate: sequel must produce next action.

## motivation-reaction-continuity
Trigger: characters act because author needs plot

Rule: Link each action to prior motivation and each reaction to observed stimulus.

SOP gate: draft gate: reject uncaused actions.

## desire-danger-tension
Trigger: chapter has no forward pull

Rule: Pair desire with danger; reader must know what is wanted and what threatens it.

SOP gate: story_engine gate: want and cost cannot be abstract.

## viewpoint-pressure
Trigger: POV lacks immediacy

Rule: Filter information through what the viewpoint character notices under pressure.

SOP gate: draft gate: viewpoint detail must serve pressure.

## conflict-as-resistance
Trigger: obstacle is passive

Rule: Make opposition resist the goal through person, rule, environment, lack, or inner contradiction.

SOP gate: scene_card gate: obstacle must force tactic change.

## decision-creates-next-scene
Trigger: chapters feel episodic

Rule: End sequel/scene with a decision that launches the next scene goal.

SOP gate: chapter_spec gate: closing turn must generate next question/action.
