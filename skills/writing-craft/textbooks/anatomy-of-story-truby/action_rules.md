# Action Rules

## designing-principle
Trigger: story premise is just setup

Rule: Find the organizing principle that makes events feel like one organism.

SOP gate: compass gate: project north star required.

## weakness-need
Trigger: protagonist has goal but no flaw/need

Rule: Give hero psychological/moral weakness that the plot pressures.

SOP gate: character_engine gate: gap/need required.

## desire-line
Trigger: plot lacks spine

Rule: Define one visible desire line that organizes action.

SOP gate: story_engine gate: external want required.

## opponent-as-necessary
Trigger: villain is obstacle only

Rule: Opponent should attack hero weakness/desire and represent competing values.

SOP gate: character_engine gate: opposition maps to protagonist gap.

## character-web
Trigger: supporting cast random

Rule: Design each major character as variation/contrast on the central problem.

SOP gate: character_engine gate: relationship function required.

## moral-argument
Trigger: theme preached

Rule: Let characters enact competing strategies so action argues the theme.

SOP gate: theme_action gate: at least three tested situations.

## plan-and-counterplan
Trigger: middle wanders

Rule: Hero plan and opponent counterplan should drive sequence turns.

SOP gate: volume_structure gate: sequence goals/counterpressure required.

## self-revelation
Trigger: ending changes facts only

Rule: End with recognition, refusal, or changed self-knowledge tied to action cost.

SOP gate: revision gate: final outcome maps to need.
