# Diagnostics

## Symptom: story premise is just setup
Repair: Find the organizing principle that makes events feel like one organism.
Gate: compass gate: project north star required.

## Symptom: protagonist has goal but no flaw/need
Repair: Give hero psychological/moral weakness that the plot pressures.
Gate: character_engine gate: gap/need required.

## Symptom: plot lacks spine
Repair: Define one visible desire line that organizes action.
Gate: story_engine gate: external want required.

## Symptom: villain is obstacle only
Repair: Opponent should attack hero weakness/desire and represent competing values.
Gate: character_engine gate: opposition maps to protagonist gap.

## Symptom: supporting cast random
Repair: Design each major character as variation/contrast on the central problem.
Gate: character_engine gate: relationship function required.

## Symptom: theme preached
Repair: Let characters enact competing strategies so action argues the theme.
Gate: theme_action gate: at least three tested situations.

## Symptom: middle wanders
Repair: Hero plan and opponent counterplan should drive sequence turns.
Gate: volume_structure gate: sequence goals/counterpressure required.

## Symptom: ending changes facts only
Repair: End with recognition, refusal, or changed self-knowledge tied to action cost.
Gate: revision gate: final outcome maps to need.
