# Action Rules

## honest-observation
Trigger: description feels generic

Rule: Write from concrete observation; choose telling details that reveal situation.

SOP gate: draft gate: description has story function.

## toolbox
Trigger: basic craft neglected

Rule: Maintain toolbox: vocabulary, grammar, paragraph, dialogue, revision.

SOP gate: prose_audit gate: basic craft pass.

## situation-first
Trigger: plot feels forced

Rule: Start from situation and character pressure, then let causality develop.

SOP gate: compass/story_engine gate: situation plus pressure.

## description-selection
Trigger: description over/underwritten

Rule: Describe enough to seed reader imagination; avoid catalogues.

SOP gate: prose_audit gate: description proportion.

## dialogue-honesty
Trigger: dialogue too polished

Rule: Let dialogue reflect character, context, and pressure, not author correctness.

SOP gate: dialogue_map gate: voice pressure.

## second-draft-equals-first-minus
Trigger: draft bloated

Rule: Cut redundancy and tighten after full layer diagnosis.

SOP gate: revision gate: compression pass.

## read-write-discipline
Trigger: craft detached from reading

Rule: Use broad reading and steady writing as craft input; convert observations into rules.

SOP gate: revision gate: lesson captured.

## avoid-adverbial-explaining
Trigger: dialogue tags explain emotion

Rule: Prefer context/action/voice to explanatory adverbs in dialogue.

SOP gate: prose_audit gate: tag/adverb audit.
