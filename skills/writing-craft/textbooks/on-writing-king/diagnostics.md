# Diagnostics

## Symptom: description feels generic
Repair: Write from concrete observation; choose telling details that reveal situation.
Gate: draft gate: description has story function.

## Symptom: basic craft neglected
Repair: Maintain toolbox: vocabulary, grammar, paragraph, dialogue, revision.
Gate: prose_audit gate: basic craft pass.

## Symptom: plot feels forced
Repair: Start from situation and character pressure, then let causality develop.
Gate: compass/story_engine gate: situation plus pressure.

## Symptom: description over/underwritten
Repair: Describe enough to seed reader imagination; avoid catalogues.
Gate: prose_audit gate: description proportion.

## Symptom: dialogue too polished
Repair: Let dialogue reflect character, context, and pressure, not author correctness.
Gate: dialogue_map gate: voice pressure.

## Symptom: draft bloated
Repair: Cut redundancy and tighten after full layer diagnosis.
Gate: revision gate: compression pass.

## Symptom: craft detached from reading
Repair: Use broad reading and steady writing as craft input; convert observations into rules.
Gate: revision gate: lesson captured.

## Symptom: dialogue tags explain emotion
Repair: Prefer context/action/voice to explanatory adverbs in dialogue.
Gate: prose_audit gate: tag/adverb audit.
