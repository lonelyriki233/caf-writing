# Diagnostics

## Symptom: opening explains background
Repair: Open with character under pressure; delay explanation until action needs it.
Gate: draft gate: no encyclopedia opening.

## Symptom: plot resolves by convenience
Repair: Increase resistance and cost; avoid easy coincidence solutions.
Gate: story_engine gate: obstacle must actively resist.

## Symptom: scene pauses for lecture
Repair: Convert explanation into conflict, task, correction, or discovery.
Gate: prose_audit gate: exposition must be dramatized.

## Symptom: dialogue sounds conversational but pointless
Repair: Dialogue must pursue, hide, pressure, refuse, seduce, bargain, or reveal.
Gate: dialogue_map gate: action verb per speaker.

## Symptom: character choice feels arbitrary
Repair: Show motive, pressure, or mistaken belief before major choice.
Gate: character_engine gate: choice maps to want/need/wrong strategy.

## Symptom: hero avoids real loss
Repair: Let choices produce cost, loss, embarrassment, obligation, or danger.
Gate: story_engine gate: failure cost required.

## Symptom: draft treated as final
Repair: Revise by layer: structure, scene, dialogue, prose, not all at once.
Gate: revision gate: name changed layer.

## Symptom: nothing changes
Repair: Each scene must alter information, relationship, plan, danger, or emotional commitment.
Gate: scene_card gate: changed situation required.
