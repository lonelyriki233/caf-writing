# Diagnostics

## Symptom: prose rambles
Repair: Treat each sentence as a unit of perception/thought/action.
Gate: prose_audit gate: sentence purpose clear.

## Symptom: long sentences blur pressure
Repair: Use shorter sentences to reveal attention, turn, or impact, not as gimmick.
Gate: draft gate: sentence length tied to pressure.

## Symptom: prose padded
Repair: Cut throat-clearing, generic intensifiers, and pre-explanations.
Gate: revision gate: filler removed.

## Symptom: description diffuse
Repair: Let a sentence notice one exact thing that matters.
Gate: draft gate: concrete perception.

## Symptom: editing only polishes grammar
Repair: Revision means seeing what each sentence actually does.
Gate: revision gate: function audit.

## Symptom: attention unfocused
Repair: Sequence sentences to guide reader attention step by step.
Gate: prose_audit gate: attention flow.

## Symptom: rules become mechanical
Repair: Use craft rules to sharpen perception, not to produce formulaic prose.
Gate: compass gate: rule purpose named.

## Symptom: prose overexplains
Repair: Let shortness, silence, and juxtaposition carry meaning when appropriate.
Gate: prose_audit gate: avoid overexplaining.
