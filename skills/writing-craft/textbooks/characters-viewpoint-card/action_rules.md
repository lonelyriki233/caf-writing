# Action Rules

## character-function
Trigger: characters exist without story job

Rule: Define whether character drives plot, reflects theme, pressures hero, or colors world.

SOP gate: character_engine gate: relationship/function required.

## mice-quotient
Trigger: story promise unclear

Rule: Clarify whether story promise is milieu, idea, character, or event dominant.

SOP gate: compass gate: story mode named.

## sympathy-interest
Trigger: reader investment weak

Rule: Build interest through competence, vulnerability, mystery, conflict, or moral pressure.

SOP gate: draft gate: intro has investment hook.

## viewpoint-selection
Trigger: wrong POV drains tension

Rule: Choose POV with most at stake, most ignorance, or most pressure for the scene.

SOP gate: scene_card gate: POV justification.

## character-through-choice
Trigger: description replaces character

Rule: Reveal character by decisions under constraint.

SOP gate: scene_card gate: choice under pressure.

## attitude-and-voice
Trigger: voices interchangeable

Rule: Voice comes from attitude, education, status, desire, fear, and situation.

SOP gate: dialogue_map gate: voice card required.

## minor-character-economy
Trigger: minor cast bloats

Rule: Combine/remove minor characters without unique pressure function.

SOP gate: revision gate: cast economy check.

## reader-promise
Trigger: opening sells wrong story

Rule: Opening must signal the dominant kind of story experience honestly.

SOP gate: compass gate: promise matches execution.
