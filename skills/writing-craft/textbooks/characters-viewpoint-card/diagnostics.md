# Diagnostics

## Symptom: characters exist without story job
Repair: Define whether character drives plot, reflects theme, pressures hero, or colors world.
Gate: character_engine gate: relationship/function required.

## Symptom: story promise unclear
Repair: Clarify whether story promise is milieu, idea, character, or event dominant.
Gate: compass gate: story mode named.

## Symptom: reader investment weak
Repair: Build interest through competence, vulnerability, mystery, conflict, or moral pressure.
Gate: draft gate: intro has investment hook.

## Symptom: wrong POV drains tension
Repair: Choose POV with most at stake, most ignorance, or most pressure for the scene.
Gate: scene_card gate: POV justification.

## Symptom: description replaces character
Repair: Reveal character by decisions under constraint.
Gate: scene_card gate: choice under pressure.

## Symptom: voices interchangeable
Repair: Voice comes from attitude, education, status, desire, fear, and situation.
Gate: dialogue_map gate: voice card required.

## Symptom: minor cast bloats
Repair: Combine/remove minor characters without unique pressure function.
Gate: revision gate: cast economy check.

## Symptom: opening sells wrong story
Repair: Opening must signal the dominant kind of story experience honestly.
Gate: compass gate: promise matches execution.
