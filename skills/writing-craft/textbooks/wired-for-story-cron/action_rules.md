# Action Rules

## story-as-meaning
Trigger: events happen but do not matter

Rule: Make every event meaningful to protagonist’s internal issue and goal.

SOP gate: story_engine gate: why it matters required.

## specificity
Trigger: setup is generic

Rule: Use specific concrete details that reveal motive, stakes, or misbelief.

SOP gate: draft gate: detail must carry story function.

## internal-issue
Trigger: plot external only

Rule: Identify internal issue that makes external events emotionally consequential.

SOP gate: character_engine gate: gap/internal issue.

## cause-effect
Trigger: episodes disconnected

Rule: Each event should cause the next through decision, consequence, or revelation.

SOP gate: volume_structure gate: causal chain.

## stakes-personal
Trigger: stakes big but impersonal

Rule: Translate stakes into personal consequence for protagonist.

SOP gate: story_engine gate: failure cost personal.

## reader-question
Trigger: chapter lacks curiosity

Rule: Plant clear what-will-happen/why-it-matters questions.

SOP gate: chapter_spec gate: new question required.

## misbelief-in-action
Trigger: flaw told not shown

Rule: Show misbelief through choices under pressure.

SOP gate: scene_card gate: tactic reveals belief.

## emotion-from-context
Trigger: emotion label feels cheap

Rule: Build emotion from context, expectation, gap, and consequence.

SOP gate: prose_audit gate: emotion embodied.
