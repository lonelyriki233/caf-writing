# Diagnostics

## Symptom: events happen but do not matter
Repair: Make every event meaningful to protagonist’s internal issue and goal.
Gate: story_engine gate: why it matters required.

## Symptom: setup is generic
Repair: Use specific concrete details that reveal motive, stakes, or misbelief.
Gate: draft gate: detail must carry story function.

## Symptom: plot external only
Repair: Identify internal issue that makes external events emotionally consequential.
Gate: character_engine gate: gap/internal issue.

## Symptom: episodes disconnected
Repair: Each event should cause the next through decision, consequence, or revelation.
Gate: volume_structure gate: causal chain.

## Symptom: stakes big but impersonal
Repair: Translate stakes into personal consequence for protagonist.
Gate: story_engine gate: failure cost personal.

## Symptom: chapter lacks curiosity
Repair: Plant clear what-will-happen/why-it-matters questions.
Gate: chapter_spec gate: new question required.

## Symptom: flaw told not shown
Repair: Show misbelief through choices under pressure.
Gate: scene_card gate: tactic reveals belief.

## Symptom: emotion label feels cheap
Repair: Build emotion from context, expectation, gap, and consequence.
Gate: prose_audit gate: emotion embodied.
