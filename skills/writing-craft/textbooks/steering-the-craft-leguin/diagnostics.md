# Diagnostics

## Symptom: prose feels flat
Repair: Revise for sound, cadence, stress, and silence, not only information.
Gate: prose_audit gate: rhythm pass required.

## Symptom: sentences all same shape
Repair: Use punctuation and sentence length to control breath and emphasis.
Gate: draft gate: rhythm varies with pressure.

## Symptom: abstraction dominates
Repair: Prefer concrete nouns/verbs when grounding action or emotion.
Gate: prose_audit gate: abstract passages grounded.

## Symptom: narration drifts
Repair: Control who perceives, what is known, and distance from events.
Gate: draft gate: POV consistency.

## Symptom: explanation too direct
Repair: Let exposition emerge through voice, image, action, and omission.
Gate: prose_audit gate: info dramatized.

## Symptom: scene pace wrong
Repair: Choose close detail or leap/summary based on narrative pressure.
Gate: revision gate: pace mode intentional.

## Symptom: voice generic
Repair: Read prose aloud or rhythm-check to detect false smoothness.
Gate: prose_audit gate: sound check.

## Symptom: style advice vague
Repair: Turn prose weaknesses into specific exercises: rhythm, POV, sentence length, description.
Gate: revision gate: exercise target named.
