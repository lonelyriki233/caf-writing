# Action Rules

## sentence-sound
Trigger: prose feels flat

Rule: Revise for sound, cadence, stress, and silence, not only information.

SOP gate: prose_audit gate: rhythm pass required.

## punctuation-rhythm
Trigger: sentences all same shape

Rule: Use punctuation and sentence length to control breath and emphasis.

SOP gate: draft gate: rhythm varies with pressure.

## concrete-language
Trigger: abstraction dominates

Rule: Prefer concrete nouns/verbs when grounding action or emotion.

SOP gate: prose_audit gate: abstract passages grounded.

## pov-control
Trigger: narration drifts

Rule: Control who perceives, what is known, and distance from events.

SOP gate: draft gate: POV consistency.

## indirect-exposition
Trigger: explanation too direct

Rule: Let exposition emerge through voice, image, action, and omission.

SOP gate: prose_audit gate: info dramatized.

## crowding-leaping
Trigger: scene pace wrong

Rule: Choose close detail or leap/summary based on narrative pressure.

SOP gate: revision gate: pace mode intentional.

## voice-as-ear
Trigger: voice generic

Rule: Read prose aloud or rhythm-check to detect false smoothness.

SOP gate: prose_audit gate: sound check.

## exercise-driven-revision
Trigger: style advice vague

Rule: Turn prose weaknesses into specific exercises: rhythm, POV, sentence length, description.

SOP gate: revision gate: exercise target named.
