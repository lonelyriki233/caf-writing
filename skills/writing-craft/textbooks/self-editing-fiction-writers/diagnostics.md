# Diagnostics

## Symptom: telling replaces drama
Repair: Tell for compression; show where pressure, choice, emotion, or turn matters.
Gate: revision gate: show/tell choice justified.

## Symptom: dialogue tags/action clutter
Repair: Use tags, beats, and actions to clarify speaker and reveal pressure without overloading.
Gate: dialogue_map/prose gate: dialogue mechanics clean.

## Symptom: minor things take too much space
Repair: Allocate page space according to story importance and emotional weight.
Gate: revision gate: proportion audit.

## Symptom: thoughts explain too much
Repair: Interior monologue should reveal conflict/interpretation, not repeat obvious facts.
Gate: prose_audit gate: interiority nonredundant.

## Symptom: prose states the obvious
Repair: Trust reader; cut repeated emotions, explanations, and moral summaries.
Gate: prose_audit gate: no overexplained emotion.

## Symptom: action beats are filler
Repair: Use beats to show tension, evasion, status, or physical pressure.
Gate: dialogue_map gate: beat function.

## Symptom: POV errors weaken immersion
Repair: Audit POV leaks, head-hopping, and impossible knowledge.
Gate: draft gate: POV knowledge consistent.

## Symptom: revision unfocused
Repair: Edit in passes: macro structure, scene, dialogue, interiority, prose line.
Gate: revision gate: pass type declared.
