# Action Rules

## show-tell-control
Trigger: telling replaces drama

Rule: Tell for compression; show where pressure, choice, emotion, or turn matters.

SOP gate: revision gate: show/tell choice justified.

## dialogue-mechanics
Trigger: dialogue tags/action clutter

Rule: Use tags, beats, and actions to clarify speaker and reveal pressure without overloading.

SOP gate: dialogue_map/prose gate: dialogue mechanics clean.

## proportion
Trigger: minor things take too much space

Rule: Allocate page space according to story importance and emotional weight.

SOP gate: revision gate: proportion audit.

## interior-monologue
Trigger: thoughts explain too much

Rule: Interior monologue should reveal conflict/interpretation, not repeat obvious facts.

SOP gate: prose_audit gate: interiority nonredundant.

## sophistication
Trigger: prose states the obvious

Rule: Trust reader; cut repeated emotions, explanations, and moral summaries.

SOP gate: prose_audit gate: no overexplained emotion.

## beats-as-business
Trigger: action beats are filler

Rule: Use beats to show tension, evasion, status, or physical pressure.

SOP gate: dialogue_map gate: beat function.

## point-of-view
Trigger: POV errors weaken immersion

Rule: Audit POV leaks, head-hopping, and impossible knowledge.

SOP gate: draft gate: POV knowledge consistent.

## layered-editing
Trigger: revision unfocused

Rule: Edit in passes: macro structure, scene, dialogue, interiority, prose line.

SOP gate: revision gate: pass type declared.
