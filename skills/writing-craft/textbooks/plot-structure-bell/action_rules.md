# Action Rules

## lock-lead
Trigger: story lacks central pull

Rule: Make the Lead active, pressured, and worth following.

SOP gate: story_engine gate: protagonist agency required.

## lock-objective
Trigger: chapters feel aimless

Rule: Give a concrete Objective visible enough to test progress.

SOP gate: story_engine gate: external want required.

## lock-confrontation
Trigger: opposition too soft

Rule: Keep Confrontation sustained by forces that block objective.

SOP gate: volume_structure gate: act II route is confrontation.

## lock-knockout
Trigger: ending fizzles

Rule: Design a Knockout ending that resolves the central fight with cost and force.

SOP gate: volume_structure gate: finale choice + consequence.

## disturbance
Trigger: opening starts normal too long

Rule: Begin with disturbance that interrupts status quo before heavy explanation.

SOP gate: chapter_spec gate: opening imbalance required.

## doorways-no-return
Trigger: act breaks are weak

Rule: Use doorway events that push the lead into no-return commitment.

SOP gate: volume_structure gate: plot point changes commitment.

## death-stakes
Trigger: stakes are vague

Rule: Define physical, professional, or psychological death stakes.

SOP gate: story_engine gate: failure cost concrete.

## adhesive
Trigger: character could walk away

Rule: Add forces that bind protagonist to problem: duty, desire, threat, guilt, love, mystery.

SOP gate: story_engine gate: why-now/why-stay required.
