# Diagnostics

## Symptom: story lacks central pull
Repair: Make the Lead active, pressured, and worth following.
Gate: story_engine gate: protagonist agency required.

## Symptom: chapters feel aimless
Repair: Give a concrete Objective visible enough to test progress.
Gate: story_engine gate: external want required.

## Symptom: opposition too soft
Repair: Keep Confrontation sustained by forces that block objective.
Gate: volume_structure gate: act II route is confrontation.

## Symptom: ending fizzles
Repair: Design a Knockout ending that resolves the central fight with cost and force.
Gate: volume_structure gate: finale choice + consequence.

## Symptom: opening starts normal too long
Repair: Begin with disturbance that interrupts status quo before heavy explanation.
Gate: chapter_spec gate: opening imbalance required.

## Symptom: act breaks are weak
Repair: Use doorway events that push the lead into no-return commitment.
Gate: volume_structure gate: plot point changes commitment.

## Symptom: stakes are vague
Repair: Define physical, professional, or psychological death stakes.
Gate: story_engine gate: failure cost concrete.

## Symptom: character could walk away
Repair: Add forces that bind protagonist to problem: duty, desire, threat, guilt, love, mystery.
Gate: story_engine gate: why-now/why-stay required.
