# Diagnostics

## Symptom: character feels constructed
Repair: Give motive, pressure, contradiction, and limits.
Gate: character_engine gate: motive and pressure.

## Symptom: character introduction bland
Repair: Introduce characters through action/detail that implies role and pressure.
Gate: draft gate: intro reveals function.

## Symptom: emotion jumps too fast
Repair: Stage emotion through trigger, bodily response, interpretation, action.
Gate: prose_audit gate: emotional progression.

## Symptom: POV too distant accidentally
Repair: Choose viewpoint distance deliberately based on scene pressure.
Gate: draft gate: POV distance named.

## Symptom: too much/too little thought
Repair: Interior thought should interpret pressure, not replace action.
Gate: draft gate: interiority serves choice.

## Symptom: cast voices blur
Repair: Differentiate by desire, fear, worldview, status, and diction.
Gate: dialogue_map gate: voice card.

## Symptom: feelings do not affect events
Repair: Let emotion alter tactics, decisions, timing, or relationships.
Gate: scene_card gate: emotion changes action.

## Symptom: scene lacks focus
Repair: Revise by asking what this POV character can notice, misunderstand, want, and hide.
Gate: revision gate: POV function checked.
