# Action Rules

## credible-motivation
Trigger: character feels constructed

Rule: Give motive, pressure, contradiction, and limits.

SOP gate: character_engine gate: motive and pressure.

## dominant-impression
Trigger: character introduction bland

Rule: Introduce characters through action/detail that implies role and pressure.

SOP gate: draft gate: intro reveals function.

## emotion-ladder
Trigger: emotion jumps too fast

Rule: Stage emotion through trigger, bodily response, interpretation, action.

SOP gate: prose_audit gate: emotional progression.

## viewpoint-depth
Trigger: POV too distant accidentally

Rule: Choose viewpoint distance deliberately based on scene pressure.

SOP gate: draft gate: POV distance named.

## interiority-balance
Trigger: too much/too little thought

Rule: Interior thought should interpret pressure, not replace action.

SOP gate: draft gate: interiority serves choice.

## character-contrast
Trigger: cast voices blur

Rule: Differentiate by desire, fear, worldview, status, and diction.

SOP gate: dialogue_map gate: voice card.

## emotion-and-plot
Trigger: feelings do not affect events

Rule: Let emotion alter tactics, decisions, timing, or relationships.

SOP gate: scene_card gate: emotion changes action.

## viewpoint-revision
Trigger: scene lacks focus

Rule: Revise by asking what this POV character can notice, misunderstand, want, and hide.

SOP gate: revision gate: POV function checked.
