# Action Rules

## hook-opening
Trigger: first scene lacks question

Rule: Open with a hook: disturbance, mystery, contradiction, voice under pressure.

SOP gate: chapter_spec gate: opening imbalance/question required.

## inciting-key-event
Trigger: setup event and commitment blurred

Rule: Separate disturbance from the key event that personally involves protagonist.

SOP gate: volume_structure gate: entry path identified.

## first-plot-point-entry
Trigger: act I lingers

Rule: First plot point ends normal world and commits protagonist to main conflict.

SOP gate: volume_structure gate: no-return turn required.

## first-half-reaction
Trigger: hero too proactive too soon or too passive too long

Rule: Use first half to react, learn rules, fail, and reveal wrong strategy.

SOP gate: volume_structure gate: response phase function.

## midpoint-reversal
Trigger: no central shift

Rule: Midpoint should move hero from reaction to more informed action.

SOP gate: volume_structure gate: midpoint changes agency.

## third-plot-point-low
Trigger: ending lacks darkness

Rule: Third plot point should create low moment and force final truth/choice.

SOP gate: volume_structure gate: all-is-lost pressure.

## climax-resolution
Trigger: climax is only spectacle

Rule: Climax resolves main conflict and internal/external arc consequences.

SOP gate: volume_structure gate: finale resolves dramatic question.

## scene-disaster-sequel
Trigger: scene chain is flat

Rule: Use scene goals and sequel decisions to link chapters causally.

SOP gate: scene_card gate: result launches next action.
