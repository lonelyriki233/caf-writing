# Diagnostics

## Symptom: first scene lacks question
Repair: Open with a hook: disturbance, mystery, contradiction, voice under pressure.
Gate: chapter_spec gate: opening imbalance/question required.

## Symptom: setup event and commitment blurred
Repair: Separate disturbance from the key event that personally involves protagonist.
Gate: volume_structure gate: entry path identified.

## Symptom: act I lingers
Repair: First plot point ends normal world and commits protagonist to main conflict.
Gate: volume_structure gate: no-return turn required.

## Symptom: hero too proactive too soon or too passive too long
Repair: Use first half to react, learn rules, fail, and reveal wrong strategy.
Gate: volume_structure gate: response phase function.

## Symptom: no central shift
Repair: Midpoint should move hero from reaction to more informed action.
Gate: volume_structure gate: midpoint changes agency.

## Symptom: ending lacks darkness
Repair: Third plot point should create low moment and force final truth/choice.
Gate: volume_structure gate: all-is-lost pressure.

## Symptom: climax is only spectacle
Repair: Climax resolves main conflict and internal/external arc consequences.
Gate: volume_structure gate: finale resolves dramatic question.

## Symptom: scene chain is flat
Repair: Use scene goals and sequel decisions to link chapters causally.
Gate: scene_card gate: result launches next action.
