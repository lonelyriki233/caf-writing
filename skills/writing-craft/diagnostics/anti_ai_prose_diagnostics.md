# Anti-AI Prose Diagnostics

Grounded in local light-novel style guides and `light-novel-anti-ai-checklist.md`.

## Common AI flavor
- every sentence too complete
- emotions labeled instead of embodied
- exposition arrives too early and too neatly
- all characters are functionally correct
- paragraph rhythm uniform
- scene ends with summary instead of residue/question

## Repair actions
- let characters dodge, interrupt, half-answer, or say the wrong thing
- move emotion into body, object, space, pause, or action
- put exposition behind current action need
- give every significant NPC a pressure and bias
- vary paragraph lengths deliberately
- end scenes with changed situation, not moral summary
