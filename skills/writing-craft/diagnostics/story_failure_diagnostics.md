# Story Failure Diagnostics

## Symptom: 设定很多但不好看
Likely causes:
- no protagonist pressure
- worldbuilding shown before trouble
- no scene result
Repair:
1. identify protagonist want/cost
2. move exposition into task, procedure, conflict, or mistake
3. force a scene result that worsens or redirects the situation

## Symptom: 开头慢
Likely causes:
- background before imbalance
- no immediate target
- no new question
Repair:
- open with a task, mistake, deadline, refusal, or relationship friction
- delay history until reader needs it

## Symptom: 中段散
Likely causes:
- no sequence goals
- midpoint does not change pressure level
- events do not invalidate old strategy
Repair:
- define 3-5 chapter sequence with goal and turn
- make midpoint change target/risk/relationship/information

## Symptom: 结尾打赢但人物没完成
Likely causes:
- no Want/Need split
- final choice has no cost
Repair:
- force protagonist to abandon old strategy
- make victory require internal change or explicit refusal to change
