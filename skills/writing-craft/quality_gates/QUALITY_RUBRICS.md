# Fiction Quality Rubrics

Score 0-3. A chapter with any 0 in story engine, scene result, or traction cannot pass.

## 1. Hook / entry pressure
0: background first, no urgent imbalance.
1: problem exists but abstract.
2: concrete task/friction appears early.
3: first page creates specific question, pressure, and character angle.

## 2. Protagonist agency
0: protagonist observes.
1: protagonist reacts only.
2: protagonist chooses tactics under pressure.
3: protagonist's choice changes situation and exposes want/need/wrong strategy.

## 3. Scene target-obstacle-result
0: scene can be deleted.
1: target or obstacle exists, result weak.
2: clear result changes information/relationship/plan.
3: result creates new pressure or irreversible turn.

## 4. Dialogue subtext
0: Q&A exposition.
1: character voices differ slightly.
2: each speaker has wants and action verbs.
3: said/unsaid/unsayable shape conflict and reveal character under pressure.

## 5. Exposition timing
0: encyclopedia dump.
1: explanation appears before need.
2: information follows action need.
3: exposition is dramatized through conflict, procedure, taboo, mistake, or negotiation.

## 6. Emotional payoff
0: emotion named only.
1: reaction present but generic.
2: emotion embodied in action/object/choice.
3: payoff lands because earlier pressure, delay, and cost prepared it.

## Pass threshold
- Draft gate pass: no 0, average >= 2.0.
- Strong chapter target: no 0/1 in core story dimensions, average >= 2.5.
- “用得好” target: at least one 3-level moment in hook, dialogue, or emotional payoff.
