# Anti-Blandness Gate / 反平庸闸门

Trigger: 章节“没错但不好看”、人物“合理但没劲”、对白“通顺但无味”。

## Hard questions
1. What does the reader fear/hope will happen next?
2. What contradiction cannot be solved by explanation?
3. What does the protagonist risk losing now?
4. What is the visible pressure object/procedure/deadline?
5. What line/action changes relationship or power?
6. What is delayed, and why will the reader care?
7. What moment cannot be replaced by summary?

## Fail labels
- bland_scene: scene has no irreversible result
- bland_protagonist: protagonist observes more than acts
- bland_dialogue: lines exchange information but not pressure
- bland_worldbuilding: setting is described before it causes trouble
- bland_emotion: emotion is named but not embodied or chosen under cost
- bland_chapter: chapter closes without turn/new question

## Repair
- Add pressure before explanation.
- Force a choice with cost.
- Make the scene result worsen, redirect, expose, or bind.
- Convert exposition into task, mistake, taboo, negotiation, or correction.
- Replace “interesting setting” with “setting causes a problem now”.
